Exports

These CSVs are designed around the job you described: weekly/monthly digest sends and marketing campaign audience building.

Digest Subscribers
Export your deduplicated send list, enriched with digest preferences, account type, business tier, and source.
Builds a clean subscriber list for send operations and manual email tools.
Weekly Digest Content
Exports fresh content candidates for the next 7 days across marketplace, events, services, jobs, and deals.
Great for building the weekly roundup and selecting headline items.
Monthly Digest Content
Exports a wider 30-day content pool so you can build broader monthly newsletters and featured roundups.
Useful for a broader editorial calendar and monthly “best of Local List” emails.
Marketing Audience
Build segmented campaign lists from `emailLog` plus user enrichment such as plan, digest opt-in, and last login.
Use this for campaign audiences like free businesses, inactive users, or digest subscribers by topic.
