Community Guidelines

These guidelines help keep Local List safe, respectful, and useful for everyone in the community.

Be Safe & Respectful

  • Treat all community members with respect and courtesy
  • No hate speech, harassment, or discrimination
  • Meet in safe, public places for transactions

Post Honestly

  • Provide accurate descriptions and prices
  • Use clear, recent photos
  • Disclose any known issues with items

Prohibited Items & Content

  • No illegal items or prohibited sales
  • No spam, scams, or misleading posts
  • No explicit, violent, or offensive content

Report & Stay Safe

  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Block users who violate guidelines
  • Avoid sharing sensitive personal information
Violations May Result In Suspension Serious or repeated violations may result in account suspension or permanent removal.
