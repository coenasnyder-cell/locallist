Help & FAQ

Here you'll find answers to common questions about using the app, posting listings, and promoting your items. If you need more help, just reach out; we're always happy to help neighbors.

Getting Started

What is Local List?
▼
Local List is a local marketplace designed to help people in the Harrison area buy, sell, and discover items, services, and events nearby.
Do I need an account to browse?
▼
No. You can browse listings without an account, but you must create an account to post listings or message sellers.
How do I create an account?
▼
Tap Sign Up, enter your email, ZIP code, and create a password.
Why do you ask for my ZIP code?
▼
ZIP codes help keep listings local, relevant, and reduce spam from outside the area.
How long does a featured listing stay featured?
▼
Featured listings remain highlighted for the duration of the listing or selected promotion period.
Are featured listing fees refundable?
▼
Featured listing payments are generally non-refundable unless a technical error or duplicate charge occurs.

Messaging & Safety

How do I contact a seller?
▼
Open a listing and tap the Message button to start a conversation.
Is Local List responsible for transactions?
▼
No. Transactions are arranged directly between buyers and sellers.
How can I stay safe when meeting buyers or sellers?
▼
Meet in public places, bring a friend if possible, and avoid sharing sensitive information.
How do I report a listing or user?
▼
Use the Report option on a listing or contact support.

Payments & Refunds

Does Local List handle payments between buyers and sellers?
▼
No. Payments for items are handled directly between buyers and sellers. Local List only processes payments for optional features.
What payments does Local List process?
▼
Local List may process payments for optional features such as Featured Listings.
What is the refund policy?
▼
Refunds are issued only for duplicate charges, technical issues, or payment errors.
How do I request a refund?
▼
Contact support with your account email and transaction details.

Policies & Prohibited Items

What items are not allowed?
▼
Illegal items, stolen property, adult content, restricted weapons, or anything violating laws are not allowed.
Can businesses post listings?
▼
Yes, as long as listings follow policies and remain relevant to the local community.
Can my account be suspended?
▼
Accounts that repeatedly violate policies or misuse the platform may be restricted or suspended.

Contact Support

How do I contact support?
▼
Use the Contact page in the app or email support at support@locallist.com.
What information should I include?
▼
Include your account email, description of the issue, and any relevant details.
